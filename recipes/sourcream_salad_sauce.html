Sour Cream Salad Sauce

Ingredients for 500ml sauce

  • Good quality olive oil 150ml
  • Sour cream 150ml
  • Yoghurt low fat 100ml
  • Mustard 1tsp
  • White vinegar good quality20ml
  • Diced onion 30g
  • Chopped garlic 10g

Steps

  1. In a pan fry the onions an garlic at low heat until cooked
  2. Pour in a high bowl all ingredients, except oil
  3. With an immersion blender start pureeing the sauce
  4. Add olive oil to mixture slowly
  5. If the sauce gets to thick add a bit of water
  6. Taste and add more salt, acid or sugar
