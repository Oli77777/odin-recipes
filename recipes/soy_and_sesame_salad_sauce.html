Asian Style Sesame Seed Salad Sauce

Ingredients for 500ml sauce

  • Sunflower seed oil 250ml
  • Sesame seed oil 100ml
  • Soy sauce 40ml
  • Mustard 1tsp
  • Rice vinegar or other good quality vinegar 20ml
  • Plam sugar or brown sugar 30g
  • Sesame seeds white or brown 1tsp
  • Diced onion 30g
  • Chopped garlic 10g

Steps

  1. In a pan fry the onions an garlic at low heat until cooked
  2. Roast the sesame seeds until brown, keep aside
  3. Pour in a high bowl all ingredients, except oil, and roasted
  4. With an immersion blender start pureeing the sauce
  5. Mix both oils together and add to mixture slowly
  6. If the sauce gets to thick add a bit of water
  7. Taste and add more salt, acid or sugar
